Foundation to Business Analytics

In the "Foundation to Business Analytics" subject, I gained a solid understanding of essential statistical concepts and data analysis techniques that are crucial for making informed business decisions.
Through hands-on assignments and practical exercises, I delved into various topics, including descriptive statistics, probability distributions, hypothesis testing, and data cleaning in Advanced Excel.






Assignment

I explored the principles of descriptive statistics, analyzing datasets to extract valuable information about central tendencies, continuous frequency distributions, and measures of dispersion. I learned to calculate the 5-point summary, Interquartile Range (IQR), and other key summary statistics, enabling me to gain deeper insights into data distributions.


Correlation and Covariance Analysis

In this assignment, I studied the concepts of covariance and correlation, crucial measures in understanding the relationship between variables. Through practical exercises, I learned to calculate and interpret these measures, offering valuable insights into patterns and dependencies within the data.


Probability Distributions

I delved into various probability distributions, including the binomial, Poisson, and hypergeometric distributions. Through calculations and simulations, I grasped their applications and how they are used in real-world scenarios.


Normal Distribution and Confidence Interval

Understanding the normal distribution's properties and characteristics was a significant part of the assignment. I also explored the concept of confidence intervals, a fundamental tool for estimating population parameters from sample data.


Hypothesis Testing

I delved into the world of hypothesis testing, learning various techniques such as t-test and z-test. I applied these methods to draw conclusions about population parameters based on sample data, enabling me to make data-driven decisions.


Data Cleaning and Visualization in Advanced Excel

I explored data cleaning techniques, such as dealing with missing values, handling outliers, and ensuring data consistency. I also learned to create visually appealing charts and graphs in Advanced Excel to present data effectively.


Outcomes

- Developed a strong foundation in descriptive statistics and data analysis techniques, empowering me to draw valuable insights from raw data.

- Gained proficiency in probability distributions, allowing me to model and predict various uncertain events in business scenarios.

- Acquired knowledge of hypothesis testing, enabling me to make statistically sound decisions based on sample data.

- Mastered data cleaning and visualization in Advanced Excel, facilitating effective communication of data findings to diverse stakeholders.
