Data Visualization in Tableau and Power BI

In the "Data Visualization" subject, I explored the powerful tools of Tableau and Power BI to create visually compelling and insightful data representations.
Through a group assignment focused on the renowned brand "Nike," I learned to combine aesthetics, annotations, and storytelling to present data-driven insights effectively.






Group Assignment: "Nike" Data Visualization

For this group assignment, my team and I embarked on a comprehensive data visualization project centered around the iconic brand "Nike." The objective was to showcase the brand's stock performance, Point of Care (POC) data, and other key metrics in a visually engaging manner while weaving a compelling story with the insights.

Data Visualization Process

Data Gathering and Cleaning

We collected historical stock data and relevant POC metrics for Nike, ensuring the data was accurate and free from inconsistencies.


Data Analysis and Exploration

Using Tableau and Power BI, we analyzed the data to identify trends, patterns, and significant events in Nike's stock performance and POC metrics.


Visualization Design

Leveraging the capabilities of Tableau and Power BI, we designed visually appealing charts, graphs, and dashboards to represent the data effectively. We focused on selecting appropriate colors, fonts, and visual elements to enhance the aesthetics of our visualizations.


Annotations and Storytelling

To add context and depth to our visualizations, we strategically incorporated annotations, callouts, and captions to highlight key insights and trends. We structured the visualizations in a coherent narrative to present a compelling story.


Visualization Content

Close Price Trends

We represented Nike's stock price trends over time through interactive line charts and candlestick plots, enabling viewers to explore fluctuations and major events impacting the stock.


Percentage of close(POC) Insights

Using bar charts and heatmaps, we visualized POC data related to Nike's products, sales regions, and customer feedback, offering a comprehensive understanding of the brand's market presence.


Performance Metrics

We presented key performance indicators (KPIs) like revenue, profit, and market share using data-driven gauges and donut charts, providing a quick snapshot of Nike's financial health.


Insights Presentation

During the presentation, our team employed effective storytelling techniques to present the insights derived from the visualizations. We connected the data points, showcased correlations, and highlighted significant events that influenced Nike's business performance.


Outcomes

- Developed proficiency in Tableau and Power BI to create compelling data visualizations.

- Enhanced skills in data analysis and storytelling, effectively communicating insights to a diverse audience.

- Gained a deep understanding of Nike's stock performance, POC metrics, and key business indicators.

- Demonstrated the ability to combine aesthetics, annotations, and storytelling to create impactful visual narratives.
